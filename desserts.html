Biscotti

Dry Ingredients:

3 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
2 1/1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt

Wet Ingredients:

1/4 cup canola oil
1 1/4 cups sugar
2 large whole eggs
2 egg whites
1 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
1/2 tsp. finely grated orange zest
1 tsp. anise or almond extract
1 tsp. of vanilla extract

Method:

Combine dry ingredients and set aside in a separate bowl beat the wet ingredients until blended. Slowly incorporate the dry mixture into the wet mixture. Divide the dough in 1/2 Shape each half into a log. Arrange the two logs on a greased cookie sheet as far apart as possible. Press the logs to flatten slightly. Bake for 25 minutes at 375 degrees. Remove from oven and when cool enough to handle, cut on a slight angle. Slices should be about 3/8 inch thick. Lay the slices cut side down and place in the oven for another 10 minutes. Let biscotti cool on rack.

Scottish Shortbread

Ingredients:

2 cups salted butter
4-4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup packed brown sugar

Method:

Preheat oven to 325Â°. Cream butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add 3-3/4 cups flour; mix well. Turn dough onto a floured surface; knead for 5 minutes, adding enough remaining flour to form a soft dough.Roll to 1/2-in. thickness. Cut into 3x1-in. strips. Place 1 in. apart on ungreased baking sheets. Prick with fork. Bake until cookies are lightly browned, 20-25 minutes. Cool.
