Chicken Cacciatore

Ingredients:

3-4 lbs Chicken Breast
salt and pepper to taste
3 tbsp. olive oil
1 yellow onion chopped
1 garlic clove minced
1 bay leaf
1 1/2 tsp. chopped fresh rosemary, Or 1/2 tsp. dried rosemary
1/2 tsp. dried sage
1, 15oz can of diced tomatoes
3/4 cup of chicken stock
8oz sliced mushrooms


Method:

In a large heavy skillet add olive oil and warm to medium high heat. Brown the chicken on both sides. Remove chicken, drain off all the fat from the skillet, but leave a little on the bottom of the skillet. Reduce the heat and add the onion, garlic, bay leaf, rosemary and sage. Cook until onions are soft and mixture turns golden, about 5 minutes. Add the chicken along with canned tomatoes including the juice. Add 3/4 cup of chicken stock. Stir, lower heat and cover. Cook at a simmer for about 30 minutes. Add sliced mushrooms and continue simmering for another 20 minutes. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
