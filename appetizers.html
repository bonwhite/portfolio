Mamma White's Tomato Salad

Heirloom tomatoes

Are ideal for this recipe and

are in season from late Summer into Fall.


Ingredients:

1/2 cup Olive Oil
1 tbsp. Red Wine Vinegar
1 tbsp. Balsamic Vinegar
1 Garlic Clove Minced
1/2 Shallot Minced
3 Huge Tomatoes
1 Bunch Basil
Salt and Pepper to taste
a Baguette


Method:

Combine first 6 ingredients into a small bowl, this is your dressing. Dice tomatoes into healthy chunks and put in a mixing bowl. Pour dressing over tomatoes and incorporate by stirring. Cover and put in fridge for at least 15 minutes. Toast a sliced baguette in the broiler on high for one minute and spoon tomato mixture over baguette. Make sure to get plenty of liquid on the baguette to soad it up. Sprinkle with torn basil leaves and eat with abandon. Make sure to have napkins near by, things will likely get messy.


Antipasto Platter

This dish is highly variable which is part of it's appeal! Choose a selection of cured meats, olives and marinated vegetables, breads and/or crackers and arrange on a platter. Serve with small plates so people can create their own assortments of their favorite items.
